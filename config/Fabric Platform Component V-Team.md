
# Component V-Teams

## Overview
The **CSS Supportability team** is structured around key technical components of the Fabric platform to ensure strong alignment with engineering teams and enable end-to-end ownership. Each **V-Team** is composed of EEEs along with PM/EM leads from engineering.

V-Teams are responsible for:
- Performing case and ICM analysis within their assigned technology areas
- Identifying product and process improvement opportunities
- Authoring self-help solutions and technical guidance (TSGs)
- Building tools to support engineers
- Driving ICM reduction
- Sharing top product issues and improvement asks with respective PG counterparts during monthly syncs
