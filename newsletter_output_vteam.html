Component V-Teams

The Supportability team is structured by technical components of the product to better align with engineering teams and enable end-to-end ownership.

Each V-Team consists of EEE and PM/EM leads from Engineering. V-Teams perform case and ICM analysis within their assigned technology areas to identify improvement opportunities. These opportunities are addressed by:
  • Authoring self-help solutions
  • Creating technical guidance (TSGs)
  • Building tools for engineers
  • Driving ICM reduction
  • Sharing top product issues and improvement asks with the respective PG counterparts during monthly syncs
